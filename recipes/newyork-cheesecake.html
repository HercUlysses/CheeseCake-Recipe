New York Style Cheesecake

A simple yet delicious Cheesecake recipe, an all time classic,

tangy, sweet and a hint of nuttines from the browned crust and top.

Ingridients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Vanilla
  • Lemon
  • Flour
  • Butter

Equipment

  • Spring Form Baking Pan
  • Stand Mixer
  • Large Roasting Pan
  • Foil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven 375F

    2. Prepare the pan by wraping foil and applying butter

  2. Make the graham cracker crust

    3. Combine the cracker with melted butter, sugar and salt. Press firmly on to the bottom of pan

  3. Make the Cheesecake Batter

    4. Beat the follwing ingridients on medium to low speed

    • Vanilla
    • Lemon zest and Lemon juice
    • Salt
    • Eggs
    • Sour Cream

  4. Pour the batter onto the spring foam pan

    5. Pour slowly and gently smack the sides to help remove air voids

  5. Bake

    6. Set the pan on the large baking tray that's filled with water halfway

    Bake for 1 hour and 30 mins on 375F to 1 hour and 45 mins

    The cake should look wobbly but not too liquidy

  6. Cool

    7. Remove the pan from the oven and let it cool in a dry and clean place

    Transfer the pan in the refrigirator once the pan cools to room temperature for 8 hours, prefrerably over night
